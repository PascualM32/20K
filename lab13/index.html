Lab 13: Loops

Challenge

The challenge posed by lab 13 was to create a loop function in Javascript that looped through numbers 1 to 200 as inputs and would output the words Fizz, Buzz, and Boom, separately or in a mix depending on the attributes of the number. For example, multiples of 3 outputted ‘fizz!’, multiples of 5 outputted ‘buzz!’, and if a number was a multiple of both for example, it would output ‘fizzbuzz!’
