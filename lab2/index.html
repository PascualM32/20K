Pascual Martinez

Hello everyone! My name is Pascual Martinez and I am a freshman studying art and electronic music at UCSC. I mainly make digital art but I love to work with whatever I can get my hands on. I especially like drawing, producing music, spinning records, and making experimental videos.  I grew up in Guadalajara, Jalisco and was born there in 2003. After living there for 6 years, my family moved to San Francisco, where I spent the rest of childhood. When moving to the United States, the culture shock was incredibly formative for me. To this day, the duality of cultures that I have experienced is one of my main inspirations. Furthermore, my home country also serves as one of my main inspirations, as I try to bring aspects of my culture and home to life in my art. At UCSC, I hope to continue growing as a learner, developing new skills to further aid me in my learning career.
